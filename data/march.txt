<h2><span class="editsection">[<a href="/w/index.php?title=March_4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a>]</span> <span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/51" title="51">51</a> – <a href="/wiki/Nero" title="Nero">Nero</a>, later to become <a href="/wiki/Roman_Emperor" title="Roman Emperor" class="mw-redirect">Roman Emperor</a>, is given the title <i><a href="/wiki/Princeps_iuventutis" title="Princeps iuventutis" class="mw-redirect">princeps iuventutis</a></i> (head of the youth).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/306" title="306">306</a> – <a href="/wiki/Martyrdom" title="Martyrdom" class="mw-redirect">Martyrdom</a> of Saint <a href="/wiki/Adrian_of_Nicomedia" title="Adrian of Nicomedia" class="mw-redirect">Adrian of Nicomedia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/852" title="852">852</a> – Croatian <a href="/wiki/Duke" title="Duke">Duke</a> <a href="/wiki/Trpimir_I_of_Croatia" title="Trpimir I of Croatia">Trpimir I</a> issues a <a href="/wiki/Statute" title="Statute">statute</a>, a document with the first known written mention of the <a href="/wiki/Croats" title="Croats">Croats</a> name in Croatian sources.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/932" title="932">932</a> – Translation of the <a href="/wiki/Relics" title="Relics" class="mw-redirect">relics</a> of <a href="/wiki/Martyr" title="Martyr">martyr</a> <a href="/wiki/Wenceslaus_I,_Duke_of_Bohemia" title="Wenceslaus I, Duke of Bohemia" class="mw-redirect">Wenceslaus I, Duke of Bohemia</a>, Prince of the <a href="/wiki/Czechs" title="Czechs">Czechs</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1152" title="1152">1152</a> – <a href="/wiki/Frederick_I,_Holy_Roman_Emperor" title="Frederick I, Holy Roman Emperor">Frederick I Barbarossa</a> is elected <a href="/wiki/Germanic_king" title="Germanic king" class="mw-redirect">King</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germans</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1238" title="1238">1238</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Sit_River" title="Battle of the Sit River">Battle of the Sit River</a> is fought in the northern part of the present-day <a href="/wiki/Yaroslavl_Oblast" title="Yaroslavl Oblast">Yaroslavl Oblast</a> of <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a> between the <a href="/wiki/Mongol" title="Mongol" class="mw-redirect">Mongol</a> Hordes of <a href="/wiki/Batu_Khan" title="Batu Khan">Batu Khan</a> and the Russians under <a href="/wiki/Yuri_II" title="Yuri II" class="mw-redirect">Yuri II</a> of <a href="/wiki/Vladimir-Suzdal" title="Vladimir-Suzdal">Vladimir-Suzdal</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Mongol_invasion" title="Mongol invasion" class="mw-redirect">Mongol invasion</a> of <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1351" title="1351">1351</a> – <a href="/wiki/Ramathibodi" title="Ramathibodi" class="mw-redirect">Ramathibodi</a> becomes <a href="/wiki/Monarchy_of_Thailand" title="Monarchy of Thailand">King of Siam</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1386" title="1386">1386</a> – <a href="/wiki/W%C5%82adys%C5%82aw_II_Jagie%C5%82%C5%82o" title="Władysław II Jagiełło" class="mw-redirect">Władysław II Jagiełło</a> (Jogaila) is crowned <a href="/wiki/King_of_Poland" title="King of Poland" class="mw-redirect">King of Poland</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1461" title="1461">1461</a> – <a href="/wiki/Wars_of_the_Roses" title="Wars of the Roses">Wars of the Roses</a> in <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">England</a>: <a href="/wiki/House_of_Lancaster" title="House of Lancaster">Lancastrian</a> <a href="/wiki/British_monarchy" title="British monarchy" class="mw-redirect">King</a> <a href="/wiki/Henry_VI_of_England" title="Henry VI of England">Henry VI</a> is deposed by his <a href="/wiki/House_of_York" title="House of York">House of York</a> cousin, who then becomes King <a href="/wiki/Edward_IV_of_England" title="Edward IV of England">Edward IV</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1493" title="1493">1493</a> – Explorer <a href="/wiki/Christopher_Columbus" title="Christopher Columbus">Christopher Columbus</a> arrives back in <a href="/wiki/Lisbon" title="Lisbon">Lisbon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Portugal" title="Portugal">Portugal</a>, aboard his ship <a href="/wiki/Ni%C3%B1a" title="Niña">Niña</a> from <a href="/wiki/Voyages_of_Christopher_Columbus#First_voyage" title="Voyages of Christopher Columbus">his voyage</a> to what is now <a href="/wiki/The_Bahamas" title="The Bahamas">The Bahamas</a> and other islands in the <a href="/wiki/Caribbean" title="Caribbean">Caribbean</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1519" title="1519">1519</a> – <a href="/wiki/Hernan_Cortes" title="Hernan Cortes" class="mw-redirect">Hernan Cortes</a> arrives in <a href="/wiki/Mexico" title="Mexico">Mexico</a> in search of the <a href="/wiki/Aztec" title="Aztec">Aztec</a> civilization and their wealth.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1628" title="1628">1628</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Massachusetts_Bay_Colony" title="Massachusetts Bay Colony">Massachusetts Bay Colony</a> is granted a <a href="/wiki/Royal_charter" title="Royal charter">Royal charter</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1665" title="1665">1665</a> – <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">English</a> King <a href="/wiki/Charles_II_of_England" title="Charles II of England">Charles II</a> declares war on the <a href="/wiki/Netherlands" title="Netherlands">Netherlands</a> marking the start of the <a href="/wiki/Second_Anglo-Dutch_War" title="Second Anglo-Dutch War">Second Anglo-Dutch War</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1675" title="1675">1675</a> – <a href="/wiki/John_Flamsteed" title="John Flamsteed">John Flamsteed</a> is appointed the first <a href="/wiki/Astronomer_Royal" title="Astronomer Royal">Astronomer Royal</a> of England.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1681" title="1681">1681</a> – Charles II grants a land charter to <a href="/wiki/William_Penn" title="William Penn">William Penn</a> for the area that will later become <a href="/wiki/Pennsylvania" title="Pennsylvania">Pennsylvania</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1776" title="1776">1776</a> – <a href="/wiki/American_Revolutionary_War" title="American Revolutionary War">American Revolutionary War</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Continental_Army" title="Continental Army">Continental Army</a> <a href="/wiki/Fortification_of_Dorchester_Heights" title="Fortification of Dorchester Heights">fortifies</a> <a href="/wiki/Dorchester_Heights" title="Dorchester Heights">Dorchester Heights</a> with <a href="/wiki/Cannon" title="Cannon">cannon</a>, leading the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Great_Britain" title="Kingdom of Great Britain">British</a> troops to abandon the <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Boston" title="Siege of Boston">Siege of Boston</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1789" title="1789">1789</a> – In <a href="/wiki/New_York,_New_York" title="New York, New York" class="mw-redirect">New York City</a>, the <a href="/wiki/1st_United_States_Congress" title="1st United States Congress">first</a> <a href="/wiki/Congress_of_the_United_States" title="Congress of the United States" class="mw-redirect">Congress of the United States</a> meets, putting the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Constitution" title="United States Constitution">United States Constitution</a> into effect. The <a href="/wiki/United_States_Bill_of_Rights" title="United States Bill of Rights">United States Bill of Rights</a> is written and proposed to Congress.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1790" title="1790">1790</a> – <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">France</a> is divided into 83 <i><a href="/wiki/D%C3%A9partement_in_France" title="Département in France" class="mw-redirect">départements</a></i>, cutting across the <a href="/wiki/Provinces_of_France" title="Provinces of France">former provinces</a> in an attempt to dislodge regional loyalties based on ownership of land by the nobility.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1791" title="1791">1791</a> – A <a href="/wiki/Constitutional_Act_of_1791" title="Constitutional Act of 1791">Constitutional Act</a> is introduced by the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Commons_of_the_United_Kingdom" title="House of Commons of the United Kingdom">British House of Commons</a> in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a> which envisages the separation of <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a> into <a href="/wiki/Lower_Canada" title="Lower Canada">Lower Canada</a> (<a href="/wiki/Quebec" title="Quebec">Quebec</a>) and <a href="/wiki/Upper_Canada" title="Upper Canada">Upper Canada</a> (<a href="/wiki/Ontario" title="Ontario">Ontario</a>).</li>
<li>1791 – <a href="/wiki/Vermont" title="Vermont">Vermont</a> is admitted to the United States as the fourteenth <a href="/wiki/U.S._State" title="U.S. State" class="mw-redirect">state</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1794" title="1794">1794</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Eleventh_Amendment_to_the_United_States_Constitution" title="Eleventh Amendment to the United States Constitution">11th Amendment</a> to the U.S. Constitution is passed by the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Congress" title="United States Congress">U.S. Congress</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1804" title="1804">1804</a> – <a href="/wiki/Castle_Hill_convict_rebellion" title="Castle Hill convict rebellion">Castle Hill Rebellion</a>: Irish convicts rebel against British colonial authority in the Colony of <a href="/wiki/New_South_Wales" title="New South Wales">New South Wales</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1814" title="1814">1814</a> – Americans defeat the <a href="/wiki/Armed_Forces_of_the_United_Kingdom" title="Armed Forces of the United Kingdom" class="mw-redirect">British</a> at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Longwoods" title="Battle of Longwoods">Battle of Longwoods</a> between <a href="/wiki/London,_Ontario" title="London, Ontario">London, Ontario</a> and Thamesville, near present-day <a href="/wiki/Southwest_Middlesex,_Ontario" title="Southwest Middlesex, Ontario">Wardsville</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ontario" title="Ontario">Ontario</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1837" title="1837">1837</a> – The city of <a href="/wiki/Chicago" title="Chicago">Chicago</a> is incorporated.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1848" title="1848">1848</a> – <a href="/wiki/Charles_Albert_of_Savoy" title="Charles Albert of Savoy" class="mw-redirect">Carlo Alberto di Savoia</a> signs the <i><a href="/wiki/Statuto_Albertino" title="Statuto Albertino">Statuto Albertino</a></i> that will later represent the first <a href="/wiki/Constitution_of_Italy" title="Constitution of Italy">constitution</a> of the <i><a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Regno d'Italia</a></i></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1861" title="1861">1861</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Flags_of_the_Confederate_States_of_America" title="Flags of the Confederate States of America">first national flag</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Confederate_States_of_America" title="Confederate States of America">Confederate States of America</a> (the "Stars and Bars") is adopted.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1865" title="1865">1865</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Flags_of_the_Confederate_States_of_America" title="Flags of the Confederate States of America">third and final national flag</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Confederate_States_of_America" title="Confederate States of America">Confederate States of America</a> is adopted by the Confederate Congress.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1882" title="1882">1882</a> – <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom_of_Great_Britain_and_Ireland" title="United Kingdom of Great Britain and Ireland">Britain</a>'s first electric trams run in east London.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1890" title="1890">1890</a> – The longest bridge in <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">Great Britain</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Forth_Bridge_(railway)" title="Forth Bridge (railway)" class="mw-redirect">Forth Rail Bridge</a> in <a href="/wiki/Scotland" title="Scotland">Scotland</a>, measuring 1,710 feet (520&#160;m) long, is opened by the <a href="/wiki/Prince_of_Wales" title="Prince of Wales">Prince of Wales</a>, later King <a href="/wiki/Edward_VII_of_England" title="Edward VII of England" class="mw-redirect">Edward VII</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1899" title="1899">1899</a> – <a href="/wiki/Cyclone_Mahina" title="Cyclone Mahina">Cyclone Mahina</a> sweeps in north of <a href="/wiki/Cooktown" title="Cooktown" class="mw-redirect">Cooktown</a>, <a href="/wiki/Queensland" title="Queensland">Queensland</a>, with a 12 metres (39&#160;ft) wave that reaches up to 5 kilometres (3.1&#160;mi) inland, killing over 300.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1908" title="1908">1908</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Collinwood_School_Fire" title="Collinwood School Fire" class="mw-redirect">Collinwood School Fire</a>, <a href="/wiki/Collinwood" title="Collinwood">Collinwood</a> near <a href="/wiki/Cleveland" title="Cleveland">Cleveland</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ohio" title="Ohio">Ohio</a>, kills 174 people.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1909" title="1909">1909</a> – U.S. President <a href="/wiki/William_Taft" title="William Taft" class="mw-redirect">William Taft</a> used what became known as a <a href="/wiki/Saxbe_fix" title="Saxbe fix">Saxbe fix</a>, a mechanism to avoid the restriction of the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Constitution" title="United States Constitution">U.S. Constitution</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Ineligibility_Clause" title="Ineligibility Clause">Ineligibility Clause</a>, to appoint <a href="/wiki/Philander_C._Knox" title="Philander C. Knox">Philander C. Knox</a> as <a href="/wiki/United_States_Secretary_of_State" title="United States Secretary of State">U.S. Secretary of State</a></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1913" title="1913">1913</a> – <a href="/wiki/First_Balkan_War" title="First Balkan War">First Balkan War</a>: The <a href="/wiki/Greece" title="Greece">Greek</a> army <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Bizani" title="Battle of Bizani">engages the Turks</a> at <a href="/wiki/Bizani" title="Bizani">Bizani</a>, resulting in victory two days later.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1913" title="1913">1913</a> – The <a href="/wiki/United_States_Department_of_Labor" title="United States Department of Labor">United States Department of Labor</a> is formed</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1917" title="1917">1917</a> – <a href="/wiki/Jeannette_Rankin" title="Jeannette Rankin">Jeannette Rankin</a> of <a href="/wiki/Montana" title="Montana">Montana</a> becomes the first female member of the <a href="/wiki/United_States_House_of_Representatives" title="United States House of Representatives">United States House of Representatives</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1918" title="1918">1918</a> – The first case of <a href="/wiki/1918_flu_pandemic" title="1918 flu pandemic">Spanish flu</a> occurs, the start of a devastating worldwide pandemic.</li>
<li>1918 – The <a href="/wiki/USS_Cyclops_(AC-4)" title="USS Cyclops (AC-4)">USS&#160;<i>Cyclops</i></a> departs from <a href="/wiki/Barbados" title="Barbados">Barbados</a> and is never seen again, presumably lost with all hands in the <a href="/wiki/Bermuda_Triangle" title="Bermuda Triangle">Bermuda Triangle</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1933" title="1933">1933</a> – <a href="/wiki/Frances_Perkins" title="Frances Perkins">Frances Perkins</a> becomes <a href="/wiki/United_States_Secretary_of_Labor" title="United States Secretary of Labor">United States Secretary of Labor</a>, the first female member of the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Cabinet" title="United States Cabinet" class="mw-redirect">United States Cabinet</a>.</li>
<li>1933 – The <a href="/wiki/Parliament_of_Austria" title="Parliament of Austria" class="mw-redirect">Parliament of Austria</a> is suspended because of a quibble over procedure – <a href="/wiki/Chancellor_of_Austria" title="Chancellor of Austria">Chancellor</a> <a href="/wiki/Engelbert_Dollfuss" title="Engelbert Dollfuss">Engelbert Dollfuss</a> initiates an <a href="/wiki/Austrofascism" title="Austrofascism">authoritarian rule by decree</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1941" title="1941">1941</a> – <a href="/wiki/World_War_II" title="World War II">World War II</a>: The <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a> launches <a href="/wiki/Operation_Claymore" title="Operation Claymore">Operation Claymore</a> on the <a href="/wiki/Lofoten_Islands" title="Lofoten Islands" class="mw-redirect">Lofoten Islands</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/1943" title="1943">1943</a> – World War II: The <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Bismarck_Sea" title="Battle of the Bismarck Sea">Battle of the Bismarck Sea</a> in the <a href="/wiki/South_West_Pacific" title="South West Pacific" class="mw-redirect">South West Pacific</a> comes to an end.</li>
